retrieval verified

AI Chatbot with RAG — FastAPI, ChromaDB, HuggingFace

A two-stage retrieval-augmented generation pipeline: documents are chunked, embedded locally with a HuggingFace model, and stored in ChromaDB for persistent, metadata-filterable vector search — then narrowed with a HuggingFace cross-encoder reranker before generation. The generation layer supports both OpenAI and Gemini. Verified retrieval correctness with adversarial testing — injecting a fabricated fact into the source document and confirming the bot's answer came from retrieval, not pretraining — and confirmed the bot correctly declines to answer when a question falls outside the document's scope.
